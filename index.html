ABOUT ME

Here's the thing...

Outside of work I am an Air Force ROTC cadet, aspiring traveler, computer science major, and a tarheel born and bred. Just like most students at UNC I graduated top of my class, and ran/played four years of both varsity cross country and varsity lacrosse, and participated actively in a few clubs, and… you get the picture. To hit a few highlights, in the summers I am a manager for Aquatech Pool Management in Charlotte, running a neighborhood pool with a full staff of lifeguards. I also held a leadership position (vice president of programs) in a high adventure/outdoors group, and I am currently at the top of my cadet class in the commander’s rankings.

My interests and hobbies range from playing guitar to backpacking to snowboarding to creating personal code projects, more or less for practice. If I could pick any place to be on any given weekend it would be hanging out in the mountains every time. On the more technical side of things I wrote this entire website from scratch (source code can be found on my github on the repo resumeSite), I have built a few PCs, including a gaming/editing rig for myself, and I am currently thinking of a use for a raspberry pi I received recently, either a diy security system or a “smart” mirror box.
